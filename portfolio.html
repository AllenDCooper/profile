Butterfly is a social learning application for instructors and managers to better connect with their students/team members--a Slack for learning and development. This project began in the Columbia Engineering Coding Bootcamp, where 4 of my classmates expressed interest in working with me to build learning and development apps. I split the group into two teams: (1) Butterfly, which I project managed, and (2) Growth-Tracker, which I coded with a classmate.

For Butterfly, I led my team of 3 talented coders by performing the following tasks:

  1. drafted a slide deck that included a problem to solve, user stories, and wireframes
  2. architected app and initiated a github repository with starter code
  3. served the app on Heroku with a mLab Mongo database
  4. created zenhub board with assigned tasks
  5. led regular SCRUM, reviewing code, and helping solving issues as they arose

Within two weeks, we had built an M.V.P. that my team presented and successfully demoed for our class. We are currently building out additional functionality and have received interest in potential pilots!
